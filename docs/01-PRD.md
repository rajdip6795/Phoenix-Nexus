# Vision

Phoenix Nexus is an AI-powered Developer Operating System that combines a modern portfolio, AI assistant, technical blogs, project showcases, analytics, and recruiter-friendly experiences into one platform.

---

# Problem Statement

Most student portfolios only display projects and resumes.

They do not demonstrate engineering thinking, architecture, problem-solving ability, or product development skills.

Phoenix Nexus solves this by creating an interactive experience instead of a static portfolio.
